The Ashen Gate

The city shimmered beneath a veil of embers, and Lira could hear the bells of the harbor echoing through the smoke. She turned the sigil in her palm, remembering the promise she made to the council: return with answers, not more mysteries.

She crossed the bridge into the Ashen Gate where merchants whispered of a hidden chapter, sealed behind gold and trust. If she unlocked it, the paywall would fall for everyone who believed in her story.

"Stories are gates. Some are locked by fear, others by coin. But every gate has a true name."

Behind her, the flare of the phoenix mark pulsed. It was time to decide whether she was an author of endings or beginnings.
